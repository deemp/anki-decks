About this deck

This deck is based on:

  • A Frequency Dictionary of German: Core Vocabulary for Learners (Routledge Frequency Dictionaries), 2nd Edition, 2019, by Erwin Tschirner (Author), Jupp Möhring (Author) (link)
  • The deck English-Deutsch (Sorted by Frequency)

This deck provides cards for approximately 5000 German words that occured most often in the corpus studied by the book authors. More frequent words appear at the beginning of the deck.

Cards contain word forms, AI-generated German sentences, AI-generated English translations, and AI-generated audio.

Related work

  1. Search "frequency" (https://ankiweb.net/shared/decks?search=frequency) to find other decks based on frequency.
  2. Search "German" and "Deutsch" on the results page to find decks for German.

Fields

  • index - The number of the card in the deck. These numbers start with 0. Cards are sorted by this field.
  • frequency_rank - Frequency rank (Rank frequency in the book). The more frequent is a word in the corpus studied by the book authors, the higher is the rank and the smaller is the number frequency_rank. Cards with the same frequency_rank have consequent increasing index values.
  • part_of_speech_short - One of these:
    • A part of speech or its abbreviation.
    • Possible articles of a noun.
  • word_de - A German word.
  • word_forms_de - Forms of the word:
    • For a noun - its plural form.
    • For a verb - its principal parts.
    • For a pronoun (not any) - its declensions.
  • sentence_de - A complete and short (30 to 40 characters long) German sentence that includes the German word.
  • word_translations_en - Translation(s) of the word in English.
  • word_en - One of the translations in word_translations_en. May be empty if word_translations_en is empty.
  • sentence_en - A translation of the German sentence to English. The translation includes word_en.
  • part_of_speech - Part of speech.
  • sentence_original_de - The German sentence from the book.
  • sentence_original_en - The English sentence from the book.
  • word_sound_de - Audio of word_de.
  • word_forms_sound_de - Audio of word_forms_de.
  • sentence_sound_de - Audio of sentence_de.
  • word_translations_sound_en - Audio of word_translations_en.
  • word_sound_en - Audio of word_en.
  • sentence_sound_en - Audio of sentence_en.
  • sentence_original_sound_de - Audio of sentence_original_de.
  • sentence_original_sound_en - Audio of sentence_original_en.

AI-generated sentences

Sentences for the sentence_de and sentence_en fields were generated using ChatGPT 4o (August - October, 2024). I selected sentences that sounded natural and seemed to be useful for learning.

I decided to generate sentences because I wanted full control over sentence content and length. I needed control over content because I thought original sentences provided more context than it was necessary to understand a word. I needed control over sentence length because I wanted to learn sentences in a reasonable time.

I'm not a native German or English speaker, so there may be problematic sentences.

AI-generated audio

The audio was generated using the HyperTTS plugin for a desktop Anki version. I used free Google Translate voices.

Update the deck

You can update the deck without downloading a new version of the deck from AnkiWeb.
  1. Copy the data from this file into a local file.
  2. Import the local file into the deck using the appropriate note type. In Import options, set:
    • Existing notes: Update
    • Match scope: Notetype and deck
  3. Update sound using the HyperTTS plugin for a desktop Anki version. Recommended:
    • Use free Google Translate voices.
    • Create presets to simplify future updates.
    • Select all notes and update audio. HyperTTS will generate audio only for changed notes.

Don't extend this deck

Do not add new words into this deck. Here's why:

  • Since you don't know frequency ranks of these words, you can't insert cards for these words onto right positions.
  • If you add a new card, you'll need to update index values of all subsequent cards. This may be a tedious and error-prone process.

Instead, create a new deck where you can insert or append new cards.
