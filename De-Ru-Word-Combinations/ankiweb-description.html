I asked ChatGPT-4o to generate word combinations for each word in this deck.

The procedure was the following:

  1. Input the prompt: Give word combinations for German words with Russian translation, keeping each part of speech in its respective form, in CSV format with "|" as a separator.
  2. Input ~200 lines from the CSV with words (link).
  3. Repeat for the next blocks of words.
  4. When the Chat outputs the input without providing word combinations, repeat the whole procedure.
  5. Copy the lines, paste them into the deck.csv, and shuffle them.
